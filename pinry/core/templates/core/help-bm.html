Installing the bookmarklet:

All browsers except iOS
  1. Drag this ButtonPinify Itto your bookmarks or favorites bar
iOS (iPhone, iPodTouch, & iPad) - More steps but not hard :)
  1. Setup the Bookmarklet:
    1. Tap the Share Icon (box with curved arrow) next to the address bar.
    2. Tap Bookmark.
    3. Tap the last box and select "Bookmarks Bar".
    4. Tap Save.
  2. Edit the Bookmarklet:
    1. Open your Bookmarks by taping the book icon then navigate to the Bookmarks Bar folder. (If your bookmarks are not shown, tap the book icon at the bottom of the window.)
    2. Tap Edit
    3. Tap in the Address field, and remove all the text shown below:
    4. "{{ BASE_URL }}/help/#-delete_"
    5. The first word in the address field should now be "javascript"
    6. Tap Done on the keyboard or any place out side the Bookmarks window.

Using the bookmarklet:

  • You shold now have a bookmark called 'Pinify it" on you bookmarks bar.
    1. Navigate to any webpage you would like to rememeber and tap/click the "Pinify" it button.
    2. Select and image.
    3. Tag it & give it an optional description.
    4. Click post and you have just added your first pin.

What are tags?:

  • Tags are like folders, but you can have one pin in many folders.
  • You will use tags to organize and filter your pin views
  • Tags are entered as comma seperated lists. If you do not use a comma each word will be a tag.
    • If you enter: Photography, Landscapes, Alex Filatov

      • - Result: Photography Landscapes Alex Filatov

    • If you enter: Alex Filatov,(notice the comma)

      • - Result: Alex Filatov

    • If you enter: Alex Filatov(notice the lack of a comma)

      • - Result: Alex Filatov

  • Use generic tags to keep your pins organized. For example, if you are pining a photo of a landscape by the photographer Alex Filatov:
    • Good example: Photography Landscapes Alex Filatov

      • - In this example you can view all Photography, or Photograpy of Landsapes, or Photograpy by Alex Filatov or Paintings of Landscapes.

    • Bad example: Photos of landscapes

      • - In this example you can not filter by Photos and Landscapes seperately, your only option is to see Photos of Landscapes.

    • Bad example: Trees Mountains Lake Birds Grass Sun

      • - In this example the tags are too specific and you will end up with hundreds of tags that are not usefull.
